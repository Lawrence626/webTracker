About BHW Tracker

Empowering barangay health initiatives through modern management

What We Do

BHW Tracker is a comprehensive platform designed to manage Barangay Health Workers, streamline certification tracking, and facilitate seamless communication between administrators and health workers in your barangay.

Our Mission

To enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of barangay health services by providing administrators and health workers with an intuitive, reliable platform for managing resources, tracking credentials, and coordinating community health activities.

Why Choose Us

With a user-friendly interface, robust security, real-time data management, and comprehensive reporting capabilities, BHW Tracker makes healthcare worker management simple, efficient, and accessible.

Key Features

Everything you need to manage barangay health workers effectively

Certification Management

Track and manage BHW certifications with document uploads, and status tracking for all health workers.

Analytics Dashboard

View comprehensive dashboards with real-time statistics, charts, and insights into health worker performance and certifications.

Role Based Access

Secure admin and BHW portals with role based permissions ensuring data privacy and controlled access to sensitive information.

Email Management

Easily manage and communicate with BHWs through integrated email system with approval status tracking and notifications.

Data Import & Export

Import and Export the BHW and RESIDENT data to Excel spreadsheets with filtered results, enabling easy reporting and data analysis for administrators.

Responsive Design

Access BHW Tracker on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile - with a fully responsive and intuitive interface.

How It Works

Simple three-step process to get started

1

Register

Create an account as an Administrator or Barangay Health Worker with your email and credentials.

2

Setup & Manage

Administrators can manage BHW profiles, approve certifications, and track compliance. BHWs can submit and view their certifications.

3

Track & Report

Monitor health worker status, generate reports, export data, and ensure all certifications are up-to-date and compliant.
